Securing your Sri Lankan Investment

Your Office in Sri Lanka

By offering Company Secretarial, Accounting, Real Estate Advisory, Property Management, and Marketing & Research services under one structure, Silk Road Partners can assist you with your Sri Lankan investment by incorporating your Sri Lankan Company and ensuring that you and your company are compliant with the provisions of the Companies Act and other regulatory provisions all throughout the life cycle of your Company to avoid possible enforcement action for non-compliance.

The excellent business potential of Sri Lanka is now drawing great interest from offshore investors who are looking for both financial and lifestyle benefits. However, like many Asian countries, navigating the local laws, regulations, and customs is not as simple as one might imagine.

Silk Road Partners will give you direct access to highly experienced Chartered Secretaries and Chartered Accountants who will provide guidance on all investment-related statutory instruments.

Silk Road Partners develop specific services tailored to meet clients’ needs who are investing in Sri Lanka, often in conjunction with leading industry professionals, so its’ clients enjoy the benefits of the breadth of commercial experience and expertise available in Silk Road Partners.

Allow Silk Road Partners to secure your Sri Lankan Investment and take care of your professional and corporate needs, whilst you focus on growing your business and exploring the many opportunities Sri Lanka has to offer.
